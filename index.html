W5CAF AMATEUR RADIO CLUB


This is the website of the W5CAF Amateur Radio Club. W5CAF maintains the W5CAF repeater which operates on a frequency of 147.380 MHZ with no tone. The antenna for this repeater sits atop the water tower at the airport.

W5CAF also provides communication support for the Commemorative Air Force's annual AirSho in Midland, Texas. This years AirSho is being held on 16-17 September. WE NEED VOLUNTEERS FOR THIS YEAR'S SHOW! If you are a licensed ham please consider working the show.

WHAT YOU NEED

  • Contact a club official from the link above
  • Charge your Handi and make sure the 147.380MHZ repeater is loaded
  • Earbuds or headphones are a good idea

WHAT TO EXPECT

  • You will have free tickets to the show and free parking
  • A pancake breakfast and lunch is usually provided
  • The work is easy. It mostly consists of helping people out and being available for the the CAF if necessary
  • Most of the communications are lightweight stuff though occasionally a child will misplace a parent
  • It will be HOT so dress accordingly.
