W5CAF AMATEUR RADIO CLUB


This is the website of the W5CAF Amateur Radio Club. W5CAF's missions are to provide communications at the annual air show held at the Midland International Air and Space Port in Midland, Texas and to maintain the W5CAF repeater which operates on a frequency of 147.380 MHZ with no tone. The antenna for this repeater sits atop the water tower at the airport.
